FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NoteGenius AI.

How accurate is the AI summarization?
Our AI achieves 98% accuracy in understanding and summarizing content. We use state-of-the-art language models trained on millions of documents to ensure high-quality, contextually accurate summaries that capture the essential information.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely! We use bank-level 256-bit encryption for all data. Your notes are never shared or sold. We're GDPR and CCPA compliant. Enterprise plans include additional security features like SSO and advanced access controls.
Can I use NoteGenius on multiple devices?
Yes! NoteGenius works seamlessly across all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. Your notes and summaries sync automatically in real-time. We also offer browser extensions for Chrome, Firefox, and Safari.
What file formats are supported?
We support PDF, DOCX, TXT, RTF, and MD files. You can also paste text directly, import from Google Docs, Notion, Evernote, and OneNote. Pro users can process images with OCR technology.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. We also offer a free forever plan with basic features.
